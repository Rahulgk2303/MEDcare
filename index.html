FAQ

Question 1: What services does your healthcare center provide?

Answer: Our healthcare center offers a wide range of services, including general medical consultations, specialized treatments, laboratory tests, vaccinations, preventive care, and more. Please visit our Services page for a detailed list of the services we provide.

Question 2: How can I schedule an appointment?

Answer: To schedule an appointment, you can either call our clinic at [phone number] or use our online appointment booking system on our website. Simply select the preferred date and time, provide your contact details, and our staff will confirm your appointment.

Question 3: Do you accept health insurance?

Answer: Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. However, coverage may vary depending on your specific insurance provider and policy. We recommend contacting your insurance company to confirm coverage details before your visit. Our staff can also assist you in verifying insurance coverage.

Question 4: Can I get prescription refills without an appointment?

Answer: In general, prescription refills require a consultation with one of our healthcare providers. This allows us to review your medical history, assess your current condition, and ensure that the prescription is appropriate for your needs. We encourage you to schedule an appointment for prescription refills to receive proper medical care.

Question 5: What are your clinic's operating hours?

Answer: Our clinic is open from Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays. Please note that appointment availability may vary, so it's best to schedule your visit in advance to secure your preferred time slot.
